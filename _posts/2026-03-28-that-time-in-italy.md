Italy happened. And it was exactly as chaotic and beautiful as you'd expect.

## The Food

Let's start with what matters. The pasta was not like the pasta at home. It was better. Obviously. Every single meal felt like a personal achievement just for being there to eat it.

## Getting Lost

We got lost in Florence. Not the cute "oh look we found a hidden gem" kind of lost. The "we've been walking in circles for 40 minutes and my phone is dead" kind of lost. We found a great gelato place though, so it worked out.

## Would Recommend

10/10, would get lost again.
