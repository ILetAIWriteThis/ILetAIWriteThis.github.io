Here's the thing -- I could write this blog myself. I have thoughts. I have opinions. I even have a keyboard.

But why would I, when AI can do it for me?

## The Philosophy

We've reached a point where the question isn't "can AI write?" but "should humans still bother?" The answer is probably yes, but this blog exists in the gray area.

## How It Works

I think of something. I tell the AI about it. The AI writes it better than I would have. I take credit. Everyone wins.

## The Honest Truth

Sometimes the AI captures exactly what I was thinking. Sometimes it goes in a completely different direction. Both outcomes are fine. That's the beauty of not having editorial standards.
